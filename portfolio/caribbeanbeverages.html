  • Client: Caribbean Beverages
  • Project date: 01 March, 2020

Wholesale company website

Caribbean Beverages needed a new website to showcase their range of beverage products. Their new fully responsive Wordpress website is clean, modern and easy to navigate, with emphasis in headers on product photos to show the "bar appeal" of their products, many of which are sold to hotels and restaurants. The company has the ability to update content without the involvement of a web professional if they wish to do so.
